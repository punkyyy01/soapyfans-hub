## What Data We Collect

### Data you provide directly
- **Email address** — if you register with email and password
- **Display name, username, and bio** — set by you in your profile
- **Avatar image** — uploaded by you (optional)
- **Review content** — text and star ratings you submit for films and music releases

### Data collected automatically via third-party services

**Supabase (database and authentication)**  
Your email address, hashed password (never stored in plain text), and session tokens are managed by Supabase. When you sign in with Discord, Supabase receives your Discord username, avatar URL, and email from Discord's OAuth flow. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

**Discord OAuth**  
If you choose "Continue with Discord," Discord shares your username, avatar, and email with us. We only use this to create your account. We do not access your Discord messages, servers, or any other Discord data. [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)

**Vercel Analytics**  
This site uses Vercel Analytics to understand general traffic patterns (page views, referrers, geographic region). Vercel Analytics is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or store personal identifiers. It collects anonymized data including approximate location derived from IP address. IP addresses are not stored. [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

**YouTube (embedded videos)**  
Some music pages embed YouTube videos using `youtube-nocookie.com`. This domain does not set tracking cookies unless you interact with the video. If you play a video, YouTube's own privacy policy applies. [YouTube Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**TMDB API**  
Film and TV data is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB) API. We do not share any user data with TMDB. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
